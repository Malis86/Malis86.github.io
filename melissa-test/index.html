About Me:

Traveling to different places around the world is one of my greatest passions. Each destination offers a unique landscape, culture, and atmosphere that fuels my creativity. Whether it's the vibrant streets of a bustling city or the serene beauty of a remote countryside, these experiences deeply inspire me. I find that immersing myself in new surroundings allows me to envision and create a variety of scenes for my matte paintings, capturing the essence of each location in my artwork.
