Classic Milk Tea

Bubble tea, also known as boba tea, is a popular beverage that originated in Taiwan in the 1980s. It combines tea with milk, flavors, and chewy tapioca pearls, creating a unique and refreshing drink. The tea base is often brewed from black, green, or oolong tea and can be served hot or cold.
