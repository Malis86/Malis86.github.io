Meet Joy

Joy got her name because she brings so much happiness to those around her. She has a special ability to lift anyone's spirits and turn a bad mood into a joyful one. Joy is a 2-year-old Border Collie who is friendly, well-trained, and has plenty of energy. She loves to cuddle up on the couch with you. About a year ago, she was surrendered to us by her previous owners, who were elderly and unable to handle her energetic nature.
