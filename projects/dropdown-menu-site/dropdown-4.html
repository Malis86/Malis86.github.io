Meet Primm

Primm is an energetic 6-month-old Siberian Husky with a passion for running and staying active. Due to her high energy levels, it's essential to keep her cool during warm weather to ensure her comfort and well-being. She has a playful nature and particularly enjoys playing fetch, showcasing her agility and love for interaction. Primm is very sociable and gets along wonderfully with other dogs and even cats, making her an ideal addition to multi-pet households. With her friendly disposition and enthusiasm for outdoor activities, Primm would make an excellent companion for those who enjoy regular walks or jogging. She thrives on companionship and exercise, making each outing a fun adventure for both of you.
